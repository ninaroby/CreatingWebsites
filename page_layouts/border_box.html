Box-Sizing

The box-sizing property was introduced to avoid having to do all the math involved with the box model. Essentially, by applying the box-sizing:border-box, you take the padding and border out of the equation. Yeah! The only caveat is that, currently, this property is not supported in all browsers and so we must use the browser specific prefixes, to avoid layout issues.

This div has a 200px width and 100px height, a 1px border and 20px of padding applied, making the div 242px wide and 142px high.
This div also has a 200px width and 100px height, a 1px border and 20px of padding, but the box-sizing:border-box means that is retains its specified dimensions.
